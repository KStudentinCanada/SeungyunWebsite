Baking Tips

  • Measure ingredients accurately

    Accurate measurement is essential in baking because precise ratios control the chemical reactions that determine texture, rise, and flavor.
  • Allow eggs and butter to reach room temperature before baking

    Room-temperature eggs and butter help your batter blend smoothly, trap air for lift, and bake evenly resulting in a lighter, more consistent final product.
  • Rotate the baking tray halfway through the baking time

    Rotating the baking tray halfway through ensures even baking by exposing all sides of the food to the ovens heat evenly, preventing undercooked or overbrowned spots.
