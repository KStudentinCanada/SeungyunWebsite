Sourdough Bread

Sourdough Bread is a coummunity favorite, boasting a crusty exterior and a soft, airy interior, embodying the classic sourdough charm.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 and 1/2 cups warm water
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons salt

Instructions

Step 1


Mix the starter, water, and 3 cups of flour in a large bowl. This should create a sticky dough.

Step 2


Add salt, and remaining flour 1 tablespoon at a time until they are fully incorporated.

Step 3


Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 15 minutes.

Step 4


Once the kneading is done, place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 5 hours.

Step 5


Finally, shape the dough into a loaf, and let it rest for 30 minutes.

Step 6


When you are ready to bake, preheat your oven to 450F and bake for about 30 minutes until it's golden brown.

Step 7


Enjoy your Sourdough Bread!
