About


My name is Denzel, a baker from Seattle.
This is a place where I share my love for baking.
I discovered my love for baking at the age of 10 when
I baked my first batch of chocolate chip cookies with grandma.
The joy of creating something delicious from scratch enthralled me.
Over the years, I honed my skills by experimenting with various
recipes and even creating some my own.
My dream is to share the joy of baking with others
and inspire them to create their own baked wonders.
